# Exercise 4 — Your automation

Your turn. Take something **you** do on a regular basis and have Claude automate it —
start to finish, as your own project. (Start this in-session.)

There's no scaffolding here on purpose. You have everything you need: a place to build
(`projects/`), the skills from the earlier exercises, and Cowork.

## 1. Propose
Pick a process you do regularly that you'd like to automate. Write a few lines:
- **What** it is and **why** it's worth automating.
- **Inputs** (what it reads) and **outputs** (what it produces).
- **Cadence** (on demand? daily? weekly?).
- Anything Cowork needs access to (a folder, web search, email-to-yourself).

## 2. Build
- Create `projects/<your-automation>/`.
- Grant Cowork the folder and build the process there.
- Keep the guardrails: **"ask before acting,"** and any email goes **to you** while
  testing.

## 3. Present
Show it working — walk through what it does, what you'd improve next, and where it
would hit your "technical wall."

## Done when…
- [ ] A short proposal exists.
- [ ] `projects/<your-automation>/` runs your process.
- [ ] You presented it (a quick demo + what's next).
