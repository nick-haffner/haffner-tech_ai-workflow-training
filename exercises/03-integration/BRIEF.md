# Exercise 3 — Combine into one project

You've built two automations. Now combine them into **one project** that shares
resources — and reflect on what that buys you and what it costs.

## First: the lesson
Run **`/ai-workflow-training:combine-projects`**. It's a short, interactive lesson that
walks you through an example of combining two automations into a single project, with
a quick comprehension check at each step. Do this before you build.

## Then: do it for real
1. Create `projects/combined-assistant/`.
2. Combine your **Exercise 1** (inbox digest) and **Exercise 2** (morning briefing)
   into that single project, **sharing resources** where it makes sense (the schedule,
   the email step, storage, the summary).
3. Grant Cowork the new project folder and get the combined process running.

## Then: write two short reports
Copy the two templates from `reports/` into your project (or fill them in place):
- `reports/benefits.md` — what capabilities **emerge** from combining them? What
  overlaps and can be **shared**?
- `reports/drawbacks.md` — what **additional work** is now required?

## Done when…
- [ ] You completed the `/ai-workflow-training:combine-projects` lesson.
- [ ] `projects/combined-assistant/` runs both functions from one project.
- [ ] `benefits.md` and `drawbacks.md` are written.
